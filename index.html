Located on Long Beach Boulevard at 21st and 22nd Streets, about a mile north of Beach Haven, Beach Haven Fishery has been for over 20 years an LBI landmark for the freshest seafood at reasonable prices. For landlubbers, we have a delicious selection of steaks, ribs, chicken and pasta. Good fare for the kids, too! For eat-in or take out - excellent food for the whole family in a fun-filled, casual, informal atmosphere. “It’s the place that seafood lovers have made famous!”

The Beach Haven Fishery was opened in 1989, on a promise and a commitment to offer the freshest seafood possible, sold and prepared with nothing but the finest, freshest, healthy ingredients, and cooked in a way is second to none. For the past 20+ years and soon to be 3 generations of the Perrotti family, we have done just that.

To guarantee freshness, every day we receive shipments from several selected HI-QUALITY purveyors that we have selected because of their shared passion and commitment to quality.

Our restaurant and fresh market feature the finest quality local selections, as well as the finest Alaskan salmon, gourmet quality sushi-grade tuna, and much more.
